Avocado Toast

Ingredients: 1 avocado, 2 slices whole grain bread, 1/2 lime, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes (optional).

Instructions: Mash the avocado with lime juice, salt, and pepper. Spread on toasted bread. Sprinkle red pepper flakes if desired.

Avocado Smoothie

Ingredients: 1 avocado, 1 banana, 1 cup spinach, 1 cup almond milk, 1 tablespoon honey.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Serve chilled.

Avocado Salad

Ingredients: 2 avocados, 1 cucumber, 1 tomato, 1/2 red onion, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Dice all vegetables. Mix with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Serve fresh.

Avocado Egg Salad

Ingredients: 2 avocados, 4 hard-boiled eggs, 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon mustard, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Mash avocados and eggs together. Mix in mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper. Serve chilled.

Avocado Chicken Salad

Ingredients: 2 avocados, 1 cup cooked chicken, 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Dice avocados and chicken. Mix with mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Serve chilled.

Avocado Salsa

Ingredients: 2 avocados, 2 tomatoes, 1/2 red onion, 1/4 cup cilantro, 1 lime, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Dice avocados, tomatoes, and red onion. Mix with chopped cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Serve fresh.

Avocado Pasta

Ingredients: 2 avocados, 2 cups cooked pasta, 1/4 cup basil, 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 clove garlic, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Blend avocados, basil, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix with cooked pasta. Serve immediately.

Avocado Tacos

Ingredients: 2 avocados, 4 small tortillas, 1/2 cup black beans, 1/4 cup corn, 1/4 cup red cabbage, 1 lime, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Slice avocados and place on tortillas with black beans, corn, and shredded red cabbage. Squeeze lime juice over and season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Avocado Brownies

Ingredients: 2 avocados, 1/2 cup cocoa powder, 1/2 cup almond flour, 1/2 cup maple syrup, 2 eggs, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, pinch of salt.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Pour into a baking dish and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes. Let cool before serving.

Avocado Ice Cream

Ingredients: 2 avocados, 1 cup coconut milk, 1/2 cup honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, pinch of salt.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.
